Who We Are

Addictive Math Inc. is an online tutoring center with a diverse group of students that we love to teach. It was started out of a passion and love for teaching and helping students stay ahead

Jamila Robinson has been studying and researching Singapore math for the past couple of years. She started to use Singapore Math with her children and love the results. Jamila contributes over 13 years of experience in the higher educational sector. She has focused on developing relationships with students and demonstrated leadership within her Scholarship Mentoring Program. As a Gates Millennium Scholar, her goal is to assist as many students as possible in getting scholarships for college. Jamila has her Bachelor’s degree from Rollins College in Winter Park, FL and her Master’s degree from Keller Graduate School of Management.

Prior to starting Addictive Math Inc., Jamila was employed at Dalton Education – a CFP education provider. She was a regional account manager and assisted financial planners in becoming Certified Financial Planners. In 2017, she made company history with Dalton Education. She brought in over 1 million dollars in sales in just 8 months for the company. Throughout her career, Jamila has developed exceptional relationship-building, leadership, teaching and training skills that are valuable to the company.
